## üìù PAYHIP FULL DESCRIPTIONS (150-300 words)

### Formula:
1. **Hook** - Grab attention with pain point or desire
2. **Agitate** - Make them feel the problem
3. **Solution** - Present your product as the answer
4. **Benefits** - List 3-5 key benefits (use bullets)
5. **What's Included** - Specific contents
6. **Who This Is For** - Target audience
7. **Guarantee** - Remove risk
8. **CTA** - Clear call to action

## üí° DESCRIPTION TIPS

### DO:
‚úÖ Start with a question or pain point
‚úÖ Use specific numbers (not "many" but "10,247")
‚úÖ Include clear benefits (not just features)
‚úÖ Add social proof when possible
‚úÖ End with clear CTA
‚úÖ Use short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
‚úÖ Include what's included list
‚úÖ Address objections (who it's for, guarantee)

### DON'T:
‚ùå Use all caps (looks spammy)
‚ùå Make unrealistic claims ("make $1M overnight")
‚ùå Be vague ("this will help your business")
‚ùå Write walls of text
‚ùå Forget the guarantee
‚ùå Use industry jargon
‚ùå Focus only on features
